Executive Summary

A leading European logistics and supply chain giant based in Berlin, Germany, faced significant challenges managing disconnected desktop and web applications, leading to duplicated development efforts and spiraling maintenance costs. By standardizing on the Mescius .NET Professional Suite, the company successfully unified its internal WPF desktop application with a new external Blazor web portal. This strategic move resulted in a 40% acceleration in feature delivery, a 25% reduction in operational development costs, and a seamless, integrated experience for both internal staff and external clients.

The Challenge: Disconnected Systems and Duplicated Effort

Before partnering with Mescius, the logistics provider operated two fundamentally separate application ecosystems: a robust, but aging, WPF desktop application used by internal coordinators and a basic, separate web portal for customer interactions. This bifurcation created significant operational hurdles. Maintaining two distinct technology stacks necessitated separate development teams, and rolling out new features was a slow, resource-intensive process, as any shared logic or UI component had to be developed twice. This inefficiency directly impacted their ability to innovate and respond to market demands.

The Solution: A Unified Platform Strategy

Leadership recognized that a unified development strategy was essential to streamline operations. They sought a single, comprehensive toolset that would empower their existing .NET teams to effectively build and maintain both their internal WPF desktop app and a new, feature-rich Blazor web portal. They strategically selected the Mescius .NET Professional Suite as their foundational platform. This allowed them to modernize their critical internal application using ComponentOne Studio's rich WPF controls while simultaneously building a sophisticated Blazor web portal with Wijmo components, all while reusing common business logic and a single vendor license.

“Our journey to unify our desktop and web applications was critical for our long-term efficiency and client satisfaction. The Mescius .NET Professional Suite provided the perfect strategic foundation, allowing us to leverage our existing .NET expertise to build truly connected experiences. The acceleration in development and the tangible cost savings have been a clear testament to its value.”

Chief Technology Officer, European Logistics Provider
